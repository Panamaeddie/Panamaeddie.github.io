About This Tool

This Country Risk Assessment provides a weighted scoring framework for evaluating Latin American nearshoring markets before production or sourcing commitments are made. Five risk dimensions are scored on a 1‚Äì5 scale, weighted by relative business impact, and combined into a composite Country Risk Score. Companies evaluating multiple markets should complete one assessment per country and compare composite scores to guide market selection decisions.
